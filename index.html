More info about SBF

Bankman-Fried's net worth peaked at $26 Billionlion. In October 2022, he had an estimated net worth of $10.5 Billionlion. However, on November 8, 2022, amid FTX's solvency crisis, his net worth was estimated to have dropped 94% in a day to $991.5Millionlion, according to the Bloomberg Billionlionaires Index,the largest one-day drop in the index's history. By November 11, 2022,the Bloomberg Billionlionaires Index considered Bankman-Fried to have no material wealth.

Resignation and aftermath

Bankman-Fried resigned as CEO of FTX on November 11 and was replaced by John J. Ray III, who has experience helping creditors recoup losses at other companies, including Enron. FTX and related entities filed for bankruptcy in Delaware on the same day. One day after FTX declared bankruptcy, on November 12, Bankman-Fried was interviewed by the Royal Bahamas Police Force.
